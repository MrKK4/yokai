## 2024-05-24 - Missing Secure Screen on Crash Activity\n**Vulnerability:** The CrashActivity did not respect the user's "Secure screen" preference, potentially leaking sensitive information (e.g., manga titles, current reading progress, or stack traces) if the app crashes while the secure screen preference is enabled.\n**Learning:** Independent entry points like UncaughtExceptionHandlers or specific activities (like CrashActivity) must manually apply the secure screen preference, as they might not inherit from a base activity that handles it automatically.\n**Prevention:** Ensure all new activities or entry points explicitly call `SecureActivityDelegate.setSecure(this)` in `onCreate` to respect privacy settings.
