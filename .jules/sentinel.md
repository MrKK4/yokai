## 2024-05-24 - Missing Secure Screen on Base/Install Activities
**Vulnerability:** Similar to the CrashActivity, base activities like `BaseThemedActivity` and specific entry points like `ExtensionInstallActivity` did not apply the secure screen preference, potentially exposing sensitive UI elements.
**Learning:** In Android apps with complex inheritance and independent entry points (e.g., broadcasts opening activities), relying solely on a single `MainActivity` or `BaseActivity` for secure screen enforcement is insufficient.
**Prevention:** Ensure all root/base activities and independent entry points (like crash handlers or external intents) explicitly call `SecureActivityDelegate.setSecure(this)`.
